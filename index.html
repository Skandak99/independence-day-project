When did we get Independence?

We Indians got Independence on 15 August 1947. After getting Independence, it took 2 years, 11 months, and 17 days to write the constitution. And after this we were considered fully independent.

Why did we need to get Independence in the first place?

The Britishers under the guise of trade the had come to India. Then slowly took over the country, mainly using "Divide and Rule" accompanied by corruption, bribery and blackmail.

What are some basic movements that happened during the freedom struggle.

There were many different movements that happened during that time our struggle for Independence.

  • Swadeshi movement
    • This boycotts all British goods
  • Satyagraha movement
    • Mahatma Gandhi coined the term of Satyagraha. Was first used after the Rowlatt Act. During this movement there was Civil Disobedience, Fasting, & Non-Cooperation.
  • Quit India movement
    • This one tries to force the Britishers to release the Britishers to leave India
There are many other movements that took place, that I have not mentioned here.
